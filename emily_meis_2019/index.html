About Me

My name is Emily Meis.

I was born and raised in Virginia. I spent most of my life chasing my dream of becoming an artist. After graduating highschool I enrolled in Tidewater Community College. Then I chose Graphic Design/Multimedia as my degree. I've never regretted my choice. After going to the Visual Art Center for some time I realized I love to design and create Websites. I hope I can do something like this for the majority of my life.
My other hobbies include playing video games, binging movies/tv shows, baking, and drawing. The people that strive me forward is my close family and friends!
